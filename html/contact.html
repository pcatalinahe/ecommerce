FAQ

1. What materials are your ornaments made from?

Our ornaments are crafted from premium crystals. Each piece is carefully designed and made to capture the delicate beauty of the holiday season.

2. Do you offer gift wrapping or special packaging?

Yes! Every ornament from Glistening Wonders is carefully packaged in a luxurious gift box, making it the perfect present for loved ones. We also offer optional gift-wrapping service for an additional fee. If you're sending a gift directly to someone, you can add a personalized message during checkout.

At this time, we do not. However, we are always coming up with new ideas and products, sign up for our newsletter to be amongst the first to be notified of our latest releases!
